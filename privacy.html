Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-13

MiniMatch ("we", "us") is an 18+ entertainment app operated by Maesic Studios. This policy explains what we do — and don't — with your data. By using MiniMatch you agree to this policy.

The short version

  • We do not store your photos. Selfies you upload are used in memory to produce your result and are discarded immediately afterward. We only keep a non-reversible perceptual hash (a short fingerprint that cannot reconstruct your face) so a couple gets the same score each time.
  • Results are AI-generated entertainment. Compatibility scores and "baby" images are for fun and are not real predictions, medical, or genetic information.
  • You must be 18 or older, and everyone in an uploaded photo must be an adult who has consented.

We do not sell your data or use your photos to train models.

Third parties we share data with

  • Supabase — authentication and database storage.
  • fal.ai — runs the image generation model on the submitted photos.
  • OpenAI — NSFW screening and narrative text generation.
  • AWS Rekognition — face detection and age estimation to enforce the 18+ rule and reject photos with no face or multiple faces.
  • RevenueCat / Apple / Google — in-app purchases.

Each receives only what it needs to perform its function.

Age and content safeguards

Every uploaded photo is screened for: exactly one face, no minors (estimated age must be 18+), and no explicit content. Photos failing these checks are rejected and not processed further.

Your choices

  • Delete your account / data: contact privacy@maesicstudios.com and we will delete your stored hashes, generated images, and account records.
  • You can stop using the app at any time.

Children

MiniMatch is not directed to anyone under 18 and we do not knowingly process their data. If you believe a minor has used the app, contact us and we will remove the data.

Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
